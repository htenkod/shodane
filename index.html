About

Engineering first, buzzwords second

Shodane is a technology studio focused on embedded systems, firmware, and connected products. From low-level drivers to polished demos, the goal is simple: ship robust systems that are a pleasure to use.

What Shodane does

  • Embedded firmware architecture and implementation.
  • Bluetooth Low Energy and wireless integration.
  • Rapid prototyping on evaluation boards and custom PCBs.
  • Edge connectivity to mobile apps and cloud APIs.

How projects run

Engagements are designed to be straightforward and transparent, whether you need a quick proof-of-concept or a production-ready firmware stack.

  • Clear milestones and measurable outcomes.
  • Direct access to the engineer building your system.
  • Code, docs, and handover that your team can own.
